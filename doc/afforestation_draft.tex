\subsection{Carbon cycle}

There is an increase in vegetation carbon of about 30 Pg C by the end of the century.

The increased co2 drawdown from the combined effect of Afforestation and co2 fertilization seems to be offset by the increase in respiration in the litter and soil by the end of the century.

Csoil is lower than SSP585 by about 4 Pg carbon.
